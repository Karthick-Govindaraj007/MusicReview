Future Enhancements

Using more reviewing websites, or judging artists based on different metrics, could create a more accurate score for an artist. The website could provide more ways to evaluate an artist, such as judging individual tracks or how frequently an artist releases music.

Adding more features, such as music-related news or interactive quizzes, could increase the functionality of the website and its popularity. Incorporating additional databases and more rating metrics will provide a comprehensive and detailed evaluation of artists, enhancing user experience and engagement.

Additionally, in the future, we hope to expand the functionality and ease of use of the website. We could also provide more information on each artist beyond streaming statistics, such as a prediction of streaming or list of the most popular current artists.
